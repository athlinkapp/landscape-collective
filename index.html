About the Association

The buying power of a national company.
Without losing your independence.

As an independent landscaper, you're purchasing equipment, materials, and supplies at retail pricing — the same pricing available to any single buyer walking through the door. Landscaper Collective changes that equation.

We are a national buying association built exclusively for landscape companies. By pooling the combined purchasing volume of members across the country, we negotiate directly with major vendors and suppliers to secure national pricing — the kind of pricing previously available only to the largest landscape companies in the industry.

You remain fully independent. Your business stays yours. But your purchasing power grows to match companies many times your size.

The Process

Three steps to better purchasing

01

Join the Collective

Apply online and complete member onboarding. We verify your business, align your vendor accounts, and connect you to our preferred pricing network. The process is straightforward — designed for operators, not administrators.

02

Access Preferred Pricing

Purchase from major vendors and suppliers at national rates. Discounts are applied through your existing accounts — no middleman, no markups, no added complexity. You buy directly; we've negotiated the terms.

03

Improve Profitability

Lower your cost of goods sold. Improve your margins on every job. Compete more effectively on price without sacrificing quality. Reinvest the savings into equipment, staff, or growth — that decision is yours.
